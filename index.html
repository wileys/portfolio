Bio

Wailea initially learned that she could combine her passions for activism and technology during Girls Who Code’s Summer Immersion Program in 2016; she then decided to pursue computer science as a medium through which she could fight for social and environmental justice. After over two full years of studying computer science at UC Berkeley, she has become skilled in languages such as Python, Java, C, Ruby, SQL, Swift, Javascript, and HTML/CSS. Berkeley has helped her find new passions; she is especially interested in closing the gender gap in the tech field and equalizing access to computer science education. After spending this past summer teaching for Girls Who Code, Wailea feels as if her journey has come full circle—she’s ready to dive headfirst into the tech industry, desiring to learn, grow, and adapt as much as possible in its constantly evolving environment.
